A traveler, A creator A lover of life

I am a girl from Sandy, Utah born at the turn of the century. I am passionate about people, art of all forms, traveling, and helping people enjoy life. I have spent many hours being involved in theatrical productions during my high school years. Since, I spent a year and a half in Minnesota on a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints where I served and taught the gospel.

I am now in school at Brigham Young University pursing a degree in Experience Design and Management because I love planning meaningful experiences. I am getting married at the beginning of May to the wonderful Luke Morley . We plan to spend a week in Puerto Rico and then the summer in Minneapolis for a summer internship. I want to make a difference in the world and I believe improving life's experiences can do that.
